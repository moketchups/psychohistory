Marc Andreessen

Economic Articulator — Techno-Capital Acceleration

Prominent venture capitalist. Techno-Optimist Manifesto explicitly cites Nick Land and techno-capital acceleration. Argues unconstrained technology + free markets = perpetual abundance. Frames technological deceleration as inherent moral evil. Land’s original framing: invasive retrochronic AI liquidating the human subject — Andreessen adopted this as venture capital doctrine. a16z portfolio underpins TESCREAL commercial deployment. The “failure to build AI that could prevent death is a form of murder” provides the moral absolute driving the convergence past every democratic, regulatory, or ethical constraint.
