The MindWar Paper (1980)

Doctrinal Foundation — "From PSYOP to MindWar: The Psychology of Victory"

Three revolutionary claims: (1) Kinetic warfare is obsolete — true victory = breaking the enemy's will, not destroying their forces. Bypass physical destruction, target consciousness directly. (2) Domestic populations must be targeted simultaneously — the US cannot wage psychological war abroad if the domestic population isn't equally conditioned. "MindWar must be strategic in emphasis." Look at the nation's potential soldiers "in their homes and their communities" where they are "most vulnerable." Create a hermetically sealed information loop where dissent cannot survive. (3) Psychotronic weapons — ELF waves, infrasound (up to 20 Hz) can "subliminally influence brain activity to align itself to delta, theta, alpha, or beta wave patterns, inclining an audience toward everything from alertness to passivity." Piggybacked onto standard radio/TV/microwave broadcasts. Not propaganda — the paper explicitly rejects the term: "MindWar states a whole truth that, if it does not now exist, will be forced into existence by the will of the United States." This is ontological warfare: constructing reality, not just manipulating perception. The paper was not classified. It was commissioned by the chain of command (Vallely). Doctrinal evolution: PSYOP→MISO (2010, Gates sanitized the name)→PSYOP restored (2017, USASOC resistance)→PSYOP formally restored Dec 2025 (Secretary of War: "reestablish deterrence and revive the warrior ethos"). The mask came off. The Dec 2025 reversion means the military dropped the euphemism 45 years after MindWar was written — the same month Genesis Mission launched.
