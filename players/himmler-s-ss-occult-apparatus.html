Himmler’s SS Occult Apparatus

Wewelsburg / Ahnenerbe / Initiatory Order

Himmler transformed the SS into a state-within-a-state, explicitly modeled on the Jesuit order (absolute obedience, hierarchical degrees, blood oaths, exclusive ritual spaces). Wewelsburg Castle (1934): North Tower housed the Black Sun mosaic (12 overlaid sig-runes) and subterranean Crypt. Construction by Niederhagen concentration camp inmates — 1,285 documented deaths. June 1941: Himmler briefed top SS leadership on Operation Barbarossa, stating the purpose was “decimation of the Slavic race by thirty million.” Karl Maria Wiligut (“Weisthor”): convinced Himmler he possessed 228,000-year ancestral memory. Designed the SS Totenkopfring, influenced runic insignia and Wewelsburg layout. Ahnenerbe (1935): 50+ research departments. Tibet expedition (Schäfer, 1938-39), Externsteine excavations, human experimentation at Dachau (Rascher/Strughold). Otto Rahn: SS medievalist, Cathar/Grail researcher (“Crusade Against the Grail”). The SS sought historical precedents of anti-Roman, anti-Catholic “Aryan” heretics who possessed superior spiritual knowledge — validating their own antinomian rebellion. The Vril Society is a total Tier 4 fabrication (zero primary evidence, constructed post-war by Pauwels/Bergier 1960). Its structural function: bury documented institutional continuity beneath easily dismissed sensationalist claims.
