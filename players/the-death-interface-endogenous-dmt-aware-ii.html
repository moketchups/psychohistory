The Death Interface — Endogenous DMT + AWARE II

Tier 1 — Cardiac Arrest EEG + Peri-Mortem Neurochemistry

Dean et al. (2019): DMT confirmed in rat brain at basal concentrations comparable to serotonin and dopamine. INMT knockout rats still produce DMT = robust alternative biosynthetic pathways. Critical finding: upon cardiac arrest, massive DMT surge in visual cortex independent of intact pineal gland. AWARE II (Parnia, NYU): 567 cardiac arrests, 25 centers. Despite severe cerebral ischemia (mean rSO2 43%), normal EEG activity (gamma waves = lucidity biomarkers) detected 35-60 minutes into CPR. Of 53 survivors: 39.3% reported consciousness during arrest. 21.4% reported authentic NDEs (entity contact, life reviews, hyper-lucidity). 3.5% accurately identified hidden auditory stimulus during clinical death = veridical perception while dead. DMT-NDE comparison: 15 of 16 Greyson NDE scale items scored higher under DMT than placebo, 10 reaching statistical significance. DMT is confirmed NDE-mimetic. Divergence: DMT entities are overwhelmingly non-human/geometric (72%); NDE entities are predominantly deceased relatives (24-27%). Both states dismantle the DMN. The destination differs based on biological context (death vs exploration).
