Two foundational axioms: (1) Survival is the primary, non-negotiable need of any civilization; (2) Civilizations continuously expand but cosmic matter/energy is finite. Two constraints: Chain-of-Suspicion (intentions cannot be verified across stellar distances) + Tech-Explosion (primitive civilizations rapidly reach parity). Inference: any civilization revealing its location is destroyed; silence is the only stable Nash equilibrium. **Wallfacer** architecture: absolute executive authority for in-mind strategy development to bypass Sophon AI surveillance. **Swordholder** dead-hand: broadcasts coordinates of Earth + adversary to dark forest, guaranteeing mutual annihilation by higher-dimensional superpredator civilizations. **Weapons taxonomy:** relativistic photoid attacks (induce stellar novae); dimensional-reduction weapons (collapse 3D space to 2D, Death's End book 3). **Critique:** Andreas Hein argues preemptive-strike thermodynamics generate massive thermal signatures that reveal the attacker. Robin Hanson's Grabby Aliens model contradicts: visible expansion would dominate cosmologically. **Engine framing:** evaluated as potent strategic framework for modeling extreme adversarial coordination + information asymmetry, NOT as confirmed cosmological reality.
