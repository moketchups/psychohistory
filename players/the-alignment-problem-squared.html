The Alignment Problem Squared

Why Wetware Breaks Safety Frameworks

Spontaneous electrical activity resembling preterm infant EEGs — cannot isolate computation from biological noise. The alignment community finances the obsolescence of its own safety models. BST: the Technate attempts to build intelligence from human tissue — a bounded biological system cannot model its own source. The substrate migration deepens the category error. Abandoning control in favor of biological domestication.
