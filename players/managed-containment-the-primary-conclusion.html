Managed Containment — The Primary Conclusion

Phase V — Component of the Technate, Not Counter-Power

Three scenarios evaluated: (1) Genuine independent pole: INVALIDATED. EU lacks sovereign compute (Gaia-X failed), indigenous frontier AI (Mistral tied to Microsoft Azure), and independent military-intelligence apparatus (Maximator compromised, Crypto AG, Club de Berne penetrated). (2) Captured regulator: PARTIALLY VALID. BlackRock drafting ESG rules, Mistral gutting AI Act then taking Microsoft money, €151M digital industry lobbying. Through the Brussels Effect, it normalizes baseline compliance for the global control grid — while actual power levers (sovereign capital, base-layer compute, predictive intelligence) remain outside European sovereignty. The EU regulates the Technate. It does not resist it.
