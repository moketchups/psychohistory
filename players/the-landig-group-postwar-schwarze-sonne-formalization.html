The Landig Group — Postwar Schwarze Sonne Formalization

1950s Vienna: The Symbol's Memetic Packaging

Wilhelm Landig (ex-SS) + Rudolf J. Mund (ex-Ahnenerbe) + Erich Halik founded the Landig Group in Vienna, 1950. Formalized the term 'Schwarze Sonne' in writing as esoteric substitute for the legally banned swastika. This is the moment the Wewelsburg mosaic became a publicly named symbol.
The 'Blue Island' mythology: Landig Group asserted subterranean or polar SS survival. Weaponized the historical gaps surrounding Operation Highjump (1946-47) and Kammler's 1945 disappearance into a sprawling Antarctic-redoubt narrative. Base 211, Neuschwabenland, Haunebu flying-saucer mythos. Ernst Zündel's Samisdat publications in the 1970s mass-produced this narrative into global pop culture.

The engine classifies the physical Antarctic-redoubt narrative as psychological operation and memetic caching — zero verifiable physical or satellite evidence of continuous SS infrastructure beneath Antarctic ice.
