Johan Rockström

Planetary Boundaries — The Biochemical Granularity

Stockholm Resilience Centre (now Potsdam Institute). Developed the 9 Planetary Boundaries framework providing localized, measurable biochemical metrics for the macro-variables World3 tracks as “persistent pollution.” 2023 update: 6 of 9 boundaries breached (climate change, biosphere integrity, land-system change, freshwater change, biogeochemical flows, novel entities). Club of Rome member. His framework bridges 1972 systems dynamics with modern Earth system science — the pollution variable is no longer abstract but measurable in nitrogen flows, PFAS contamination, microplastic accumulation, and biodiversity loss.
