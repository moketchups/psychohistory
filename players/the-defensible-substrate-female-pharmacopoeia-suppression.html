Linguistic shift: when Septuagint + NT translated illicit magic as pharmakeia, and when Council of Elvira targeted maleficium, the operational result was disenfranchisement of the female pharmacological monopoly. Medicine woman → sorceress encodes a structural shift in societal power dynamics.
