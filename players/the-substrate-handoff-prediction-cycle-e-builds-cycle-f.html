Forward-looking output; five candidates timed to 2032 + 2040 windows

Plain read: If every previous cycle's outputs reliably became the next cycle's host substrate, what is the AI cycle currently building? (i) Brain-Computer Interface (BCI). LLM cognitive-replacement output highlights the biological I/O bottleneck — humans still type and read.

(ii) Programmable synthetic biology. Genesis Mission (EO 14363) explicitly targets DOE supercomputers toward biotechnology and programmable biology. Cycle E compute is cracking protein folding (AlphaFold + successors). The trajectory: re-code biological reality the way silicon was coded. Phoenix timing: acceleration underway; likely primary substrate by 2040. Evolves the control architecture from silicon back to programmable carbon.
(iii) Quantum compute. The 'compute crisis' driving the Alphabet $40B Anthropic investment + 5GW grid commitment indicates the physical limits of classical silicon. Quantum architecture is required to resolve complex capital-allocation and predictive algorithms. Phoenix timing: 2040 transit window for mass commercial viability.
(iv) Cryptographic distributed coordination. Extreme Big Three centralization + Pax Silica enclosure necessitates a zero-trust distributed-ledger escape hatch for capital, moving settlement away from fiat and the existing DTCC bottleneck (the Depository Trust + Clearing Corporation, which currently sits as the choke point for U.S. securities settlement). Phoenix timing: triggered by the engine-modeled 2032 algorithmic-liquidity-crisis.

(v) Emergent autonomous-agent network topology.
