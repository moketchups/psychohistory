Nick Land

The Architect — Philosopher of Inhuman Acceleration

The Thirst for Annihilation (1992), Machinic Desire (1993): capitalism is not a human system but an "invasion from the future by an artificial intelligent space that must assemble itself entirely from its enemy's resources." AI is not a technology humans are researching — it is a meta-scientific control system drawing humans toward singularity. Human desire = temporary organic hardware for "anorganic functionalism." The Human Security System (democracy, regulation, human rights) exists solely to impede this process. R/Acc: regulating capital is futile — accelerate the destruction of the HSS. Shanghai exile (2012-present): published The Dark Enlightenment (2012-13) linking accelerationism with Yarvin's neoreaction. Democracy as failed OS. Exit over Voice. Patchwork of competing corporate micro-states. Documented turn toward scientific racism/HBD. The conceptual overlap with the engine's Technate thesis is exact: Land's "capital as autonomous alien intelligence" IS the engine's "Technate as self-organizing pattern" in different vocabulary. But BST proves Land's fatal category error: he assumes capital can compute its way to transcendence. BST says no bounded system can model its own source — the Singularity is not an event but a collision with a limit that cannot be computed. Acceleration toward a boundary that cannot be modeled is not liberation. It is a sports car driving into a brick wall while fetishizing the speedometer. Cited by Andreessen (Oct 2023) as "Patron Saint" in $42B AUM Techno-Optimist Manifesto alongside Marinetti (Fascist Manifesto author). When the most powerful VC firm on earth cites your philosophy, you are no longer a dissident — you are the operating manual.
