Limits to Growth Authors (MIT, 1972)

The Prophecy's Architects

Donella H. Meadows, Dennis L. Meadows, Jørgen Randers, William W. Behrens III — co-authors of *Limits to Growth* (1972). Built the World3 system-dynamics model on foundations laid by Jay Forrester (MIT, system dynamics pioneer). Tracked 5 interacting variables: population, industrialization, pollution, food production, resource depletion. Their BAU2 scenario (Herrington 2020 correction) tracks pollution-driven collapse — industrial output decline beginning ~2030s, cascading degradation by 2040-2046. Empirically validated by KPMG (2021) and Yale researchers. Immediately attacked by neoclassical economists William Nordhaus and James Tobin (1972) — the same establishment that benefits from infinite-growth assumptions. The mechanism: not resource scarcity but overwhelming pollution and escalating capital costs of technological abatement consuming ALL available capital. This is WHY the Donroe Doctrine exists.
