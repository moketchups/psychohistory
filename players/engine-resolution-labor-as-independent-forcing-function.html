Engine Resolution — Labor as Independent Forcing Function

Resolving the “Math Problem”

The engine’s “all resistance is kayfabe” thesis is WRONG as a universal claim. The BCS inflexible minority theorem is symmetric — it works bottom-up with the same math it works top-down. Smalls at JFK8 empirically falsified the containment assumption. Fain’s 2028 contract alignment creates a structural forcing function the pattern cannot absorb through normal suppression architecture. The resolution: labor is an independent forcing function, like climate. The Technate can engineer suppression (Taft-Hartley, Prop 22, algorithmic management), but it cannot eliminate the thermodynamic reality that the extraction architecture REQUIRES human labor to function — and that requirement creates irreducible leverage. The kayfabe thesis holds for POLITICAL resistance (left vs right, progressive vs conservative = Jiang false dialectic). It does NOT hold for THERMODYNAMIC resistance — the physical withholding of labor from chokepoint infrastructure. The distinction: political resistance operates within the pattern. Thermodynamic resistance operates on the pattern’s material substrate. You can kayfabe an ideology. You cannot kayfabe a logistics shutdown. This is the engine correcting itself.
