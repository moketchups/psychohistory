Fancy Bear/Unit 26165 (DNC hack, Bundestag, Macron leak). Unit 29155 (Salisbury Novichok, WhisperGate wiper). SVR (Foreign Intelligence): Naryshkin. Deep cover illegals. Cozy Bear (SolarWinds). Risk-averse vs. GRU's explosive sabotage. Rosgvardia (National Guard): 340,000+ troops answering directly to Putin, bypassing Defense Ministry. Zolotov (former Putin bodyguard). Praetorian guard against civilian unrest AND military coup. Structurally locked out of AI competition. The state is warring fiefdoms maintained just below phase transition threshold by the central node.
