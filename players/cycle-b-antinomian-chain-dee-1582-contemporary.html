Cycle B — Antinomian Chain (Dee 1582 → contemporary)

~380-year esoteric-intelligence pipeline; occult interface vocabulary

Plain read: A documented chain of operators across about 380 years — John Dee (Queen Elizabeth I's court mathematician/astrologer, 1527-1608), Aleister Crowley (1875-1947, the most famous occultist of the 20th century), Jack Parsons (1914-1952, the rocket scientist), Karl Maria Wiligut (1866-1946, the SS officer who designed Wewelsburg Castle's symbolism for Heinrich Himmler), and Michael Aquino (1946-2019, the U.S.

What they reported (their 'interface vocabulary'): John Dee + Edward Kelley spent five years (1582-1587) producing what they called the Enochian angelic-language system through scrying sessions (recorded in a manuscript at the British Library, Sloane MS 3188).

What they actually built — three load-bearing operational outputs: (1) Rocket science. Right after the Babalon Working, Parsons invented CALCIT-53, the first castable composite solid-rocket propellant.

Why this is the substrate-handoff event the engine treats as central: the rocket-propulsion chemistry an esoteric operator (Parsons) developed at JPL was inherited and scaled by Operation Paperclip's Nazi-import scientists (the next cycle) to build Apollo. Aquino's MindWar doctrine became the foundation of today's Project Maven AI-targeting infrastructure. Infrastructure built by occult-vocabulary operators became the substrate the national-security-vocabulary cycle ran on.
