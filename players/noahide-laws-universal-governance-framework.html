Noahide Laws — Universal Governance Framework

Soft-Power Juridical Architecture

Philippine House Resolutions (2024/2025) recognizing Education and Sharing Day. If the algorithmic state (Genesis AI) requires an ethical alignment protocol to govern humanity, the Noahide Laws — already codified into US and international law — provide a pre-packaged, legally recognized operating system for global human compliance. The parallel to Jesuit Ratio Studiorum is exact: both are standardized frameworks designed to universalize a specific tradition's worldview. The Ratio Studiorum captured the educational pipeline; the Noahide Laws capture the ethical pipeline.
