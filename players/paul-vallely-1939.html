Paul Vallely (1939–)

MindWar Co-Author — 7th PSYOP Group Commander

Commander of the 7th Psychological Operations Group when he commissioned Aquino to write the MindWar paper (1980). Retired as Major General. Post-military: Fox News military analyst, political operative, linked to Michael Flynn's political network. Vallely provided the institutional authority — a Group Commander requesting "fresh and innovative ideas" about PSYOP's future. The paper was not a rogue document; it was commissioned by the chain of command. Later became a prominent advocate for Flynn and the "patriot" movement — the MindWar author performing MindWar in plain sight.
