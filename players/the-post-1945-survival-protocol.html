How the Lodge Survived the Death of the State

Intelligence: Gehlen microfilmed his archives, buried them in the Alps, surrendered to the US. CIA reconstituted his network — hundreds of former SS/SD/Gestapo officers — into a 25-acre compound in Pullach. Became the BND (1956). The lodge structure bypassed denazification via utility.
Aerospace: Paperclip transferred 1,600+ scientists. Von Braun (SS officer, V-2 built with slave labor, 20,000 deaths) → Saturn V architect. Debus (SS) → Kennedy Space Center director. Strughold (Ahnenerbe-linked experiments) → Apollo life support.
Finance: BIS in Basel facilitated transactions between Axis and Allies throughout the entire war. McKittrick (American BIS president) moved freely between Switzerland and New York. At least 3.7 tonnes of looted gold laundered. Morgenthau tried to liquidate BIS at Bretton Woods — failed. The financial layer was never at war with itself.
The principle: Secret societies are discontinuity-proof by design. They rely on oath-bound loyalty, compartmentalization, and hidden membership — not geography or constitutions. The host body (Third Reich) was discarded. The esoteric intelligence core parasitized the victor.
