Robert Bigelow

NIDS / BAASS / Skinwalker Ranch — $22M DIA Contract, Sole Bidder

Budget Suites of America billionaire. Founded National Institute for Discovery Science (NIDS) — purchased Skinwalker Ranch (Utah) in 1996 to study anomalous phenomena. BAASS (Bigelow Aerospace Advanced Space Studies): received $22M DIA contract as sole bidder (Harry Reid secured funding). Produced 38 DIRDs (Defense Intelligence Reference Documents) authored by Puthoff, Eric Davis, and others — covering wormholes, antigravity, metamaterials, warp drives, and the Drake Equation reframed for interdimensional contact. "Hitchhiker Effect": AAWSAP/Skinwalker researchers reported the phenomenon following them home — poltergeist activity, entity sightings, physiological effects in family members who never visited the ranch. This suggests an infectious cognitive/interdimensional variable incompatible with conventional aerospace analysis. Bigelow publicly stated on 60 Minutes (2017): "I'm absolutely convinced" that aliens exist and "have been and are visiting." His role: private capital funding the investigation that military-intelligence budgets cannot publicly support. The Bigelow-Reid-Puthoff triangle = the financial, political, and scientific rails of managed disclosure operating outside congressional oversight until the 2017 reveal.
