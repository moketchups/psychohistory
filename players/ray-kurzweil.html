Players/Singularity Theology

Ray Kurzweil

The Prophet — Google Director of Engineering

Google Director of Engineering (2012). Seminal texts: *The Age of Spiritual Machines* (1999), *The Singularity Is Near* (2005), *The Singularity Is Nearer* (2024). Self-reported 86% prediction accuracy. Independent audit (Armstrong/Sotala, Modis): methodology mirrors Nostradamus — broad multi-part predictions graded post-hoc as total victories. Actual accuracy ~50%, much trivially correct. The perpetual 15-25 year horizon: expert AGI predictions are statistically indistinguishable from non-expert predictions, both clustering 15-25 years from the date made. Imminent enough for urgency, distant enough to avoid falsification. Identical to millennial eschatology. Law of Accelerating Returns contradicted by: Dennard scaling ended ~2006, node shrinks now 3-4 years, GPT-3→GPT-4 required exponentially more compute for proportionately smaller reasoning gains, Landauer’s Principle sets thermodynamic floor. 100-pill daily supplement regimen to “live long enough to reach longevity escape velocity” = the material expression of mortality anxiety beneath the utopian forecasting. The prophet was institutionalized when Google hired him — the prophecy became self-funding.
