Peter Turchin

Social Limit — Elite Overproduction

"Ages of Discord" (2016), "End Times" (2023). Civilizations follow ~200-300 year secular cycles driven by elite overproduction (more credentialed power-seekers than positions of power), popular immiseration, and state fiscal crisis. Disintegrative phase peaking 2020s. Jiang cites extensively: "The conflict in states is not between rich and poor but between the have-a-lot and have-some-but-want-more." MAGA vs Deep State = managed kayfabe channeling intra-elite competition. DWM (Demographic-Wealth Model): recent peer-reviewed advancement — uses Hopf bifurcations (nonlinear dynamics) to formally prove the existence of periodic orbits in population-wealth-taxation dynamics. Mathematical proof of SDT's cyclical predictions. BST integration: the Social Limit. Too many admins for a shrinking Admin Class = the source of the Donroe Doctrine's urgency. ENGINE SELF-AUDIT (Labor Variable Report): the engine uses Turchin but ignores his conclusion — immiserated populations inevitably produce kinetic pushback. SDT requires resolution via elite accommodation OR state breakdown. If the Technate’s structural imperative precludes accommodation, Turchin leaves only breakdown. Current data validates the pre-bifurcation window: 306,800 workers in major strikes (2025 BLS), 81.9% NLRB win rate, AI displacement (300M jobs exposed). Fain’s May 2028 UAW contract expiration is the countdown.
