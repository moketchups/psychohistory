The Esoteric-Hitlerism Reconstitution

Evola → Savitri Devi → Serrano — The Theological Bridge

Julius Evola (1898-1974): Italian Traditionalist, Ride the Tiger + Men Among the Ruins. Bypassed overt National Socialism while preserving hierarchical, anti-liberal, aristocratic core. Pre-war influence on SS Ahnenerbe via Prince Borghese and the Roman Traditionalist circle. Postwar: philosophical architect for the European extreme right.
Savitri Devi (1905-1982, born Maximiani Portas): pioneered the post-1945 transition. The Lightning and the Sun (1958) posited Hitler not as a failed political leader but as an avatar of Vishnu fighting the cyclical decay of the Kali Yuga. Injection of Indo-Aryan cyclical-time cosmology into the Order's memetic-persistence mechanics.
Miguel Serrano (1917-2009): Chilean diplomat, Jung and Hesse correspondent, author of Nos: Book of the Resurrection and Adolf Hitler: The Last Avatar. Formulated 'Esoteric Hitlerism' as a coherent cosmology. Interacted directly with Pinochet regime and the Colonia Dignidad milieu. Defined the Andes and Antarctica as spiritual redoubts; asserted the Order's elite leadership had survived the war and transcended to a Hyperborean existence.
Engine read via the 380-year Antinomian Chain: this distributed network of esotericists transmuted the terrestrial defeat of the Third Reich into an enduring cosmic mythology. The ideological reconstitution layer that guaranteed the Order's memetic survival beyond 1945.
