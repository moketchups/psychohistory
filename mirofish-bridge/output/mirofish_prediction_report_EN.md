## Structural Pressure and Civilizational Transformation

**Future structural pressure will lead to dramatic civilizational transformation.**

**Key Events and Social Responses**

Based on simulation results, multiple key events over the next 14 years will trigger increasing structural pressure. In particular, the **"Fourth Turning"** predicts a peak pressure crisis in December 2036, based on the cycle described by Strauss and Howe. As society's demand for resources continues to intensify, the **"Demographic Cliff"** phenomenon will accelerate, especially in the Pacific region. As the worker-to-retiree ratio approaches 1:1, algorithmic governance will become a structural necessity.

> "The Fourth Turning predicts a peak pressure crisis in December 2036, based on the cycle described by Strauss and Howe."

**Impact on Social Groups**

Among the structural pressures ahead, the elite class will be most severely affected. Peter Turchin's theory demonstrates that elite overproduction will drive 200-to-300-year instability cycles, and current wealth inequality has reached historical extremes. This inequality deepens society's fractures, leading to **"popular immiseration."**

> "Peter Turchin theorized elite overproduction, manifesting as aristocratic capital funding populist fractures."

**Impact of Environmental Pollution**

Future environmental pollution will profoundly impact industrial and economic structures, especially in the mid-2030s, when pollution loads will exceed environmental absorption capacity, leading to industrial collapse. This process is validated by the **"Club of Rome BAU2"** model, indicating that environmental crisis will be a major factor driving social upheaval.

> "BAU2 (pollution-driven collapse) contrasts with the original BAU (resource scarcity), showing the trend of pollution loads exceeding environmental absorption capacity."

**Drivers of Transformation**

At various time nodes including 2030, 2035, and 2040, the primary drivers of civilizational transformation will be rapid technological development and social competition for resources. The construction of the **"Genesis Mission"** will accelerate due to data center thermal limits, affecting a significant proportion of Earth's land surface.

> "The Genesis Mission is being built as data center thermal limits surpass the grid, also affecting a significant proportion of Earth's land surface."

**Regional Hotspots**

In the future, Taiwan will become a potential trigger event for structural pressure, especially between 2027 and 2028. As US-China tensions intensify, Taiwan's geopolitical importance will further increase.

> "There is a high probability that Taiwan will become a trigger event between 2027 and 2028."

**Summary**

Future structural pressure will not only affect the economy and environment but will also redefine society's power structure. As the fracture between elites and ordinary people widens, the risk of social upheaval increases. The responses of various social groups will be key to future transformation and deserve close monitoring.
