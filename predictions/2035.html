Analysis

WHAT

The New Mask

Whatever configuration emerged from 2032-2034 displays maximum extension — not as genuine triumph but as the pattern reaching peak visibility before decline. Historical parallels: Victoria's Diamond Jubilee (1897) was the British Empire's greatest display of power — within 50 years it was gone. Quebec's fall (1759) gave Britain its greatest territorial expansion — and planted the seeds of American independence. The 2035 'empire' may be: the Genesis architecture fully operational (nuclear-powered AI running while the old grid failed), or the BRICS alternative having crystallized, or a fragmented multipolar order where no single power dominates. The mask is new; the extraction architecture beneath it is the same one that built every previous empire.
WHAT

Energy Transition Checkpoint

By 2035, the energy question has an answer. Either: (1) the Genesis/nuclear buildout proved sufficient (SMRs operational, Stargate at scale, orbital compute running), (2) renewables disrupted faster than expected (solar/storage at grid parity globally, petrodollar architecture eroded), or (3) the energy transition failed (oil dependency persisted, nuclear buildout stalled, climate forcing accelerated beyond adaptation). The first offshore oil well was drilled at CY133 in the previous cycle. The 2035 energy analog may be fusion's first commercial deployment, orbital solar at scale, or something the engine hasn't imagined.
WHERE

Antarctica Enters the Frame

The 2048 Antarctic Treaty review is now 13 years away. At CY133 (2035), the positioning for Antarctica should be visible: increased station construction, sovereignty claims disguised as 'scientific research,' ice loss accelerating resource access timelines. Dufek Massif (2nd largest layered intrusion after Bushveld), Larsen Basin (269 MMBO). The Phoenix reset (2040) is 5 years out — whoever controls the Antarctic positioning by 2035 controls the post-reset resource base.
