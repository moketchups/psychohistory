What is Subterranean-Genesis Colocation Hypothesis?

The hypothesis: Genesis is secretly colocating compute inside existing or newly-built hardened underground facilities. The technical lineage for this move already exists — the Kammler Sonderstab → Operation Paperclip → Los Alamos → Subterrene (nuclear rock-melting tunneling, real 1970s LANL patent) → Genesis Mission is a continuous personnel + methodology chain.
