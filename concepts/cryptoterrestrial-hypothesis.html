Cryptoterrestrial Hypothesis

Psychohistory Engine Concept — Plain English Explanation

What is Cryptoterrestrial Hypothesis?

Academic paper by Tim Lomas (Harvard), Brendan Case, and Michael Paul Masters (Montana Tech), published 2024. Proposes UAP entities may have a concealed earthly origin — (a) ancient non-human terrestrial species hidden underground/underwater, (b) crypto-civilization descended from surviving ancient humans, (c) off-planet returning intelligence with Earth-origin ancestry, or (d) interdimensional entities originating from Earth's own substrate. Fourth category in NHI taxonomy alongside EBE / ultraterrestrial / egregoric-archontic / simulation-overlord. Structural significance: the academic-materialist disclosure faction's sanitized framing of 'ultraterrestrial' that avoids the overt demonic register of the theological faction while preserving Earth-adjacent NHI possibility. Produces legitimacy for Pasulka/Kripal-style Invisible College work.
