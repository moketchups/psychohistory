What is Operational-Output-as-Cross-Cycle-Substrate?

Implication for forward visibility: observing what the current cycle is building gives you forward visibility on what the next cycle's host substrate will be — this is the engine's substrate-handoff prediction methodology. The four-reading superposition is preserved: the *mechanism* (handoff happens) is documented; the *driver* of the mechanism (whether it's a literal entity / mechanical recurrence / collective psychology / something else) remains open across (a)/(b)/(c)/(d).
