What is Topic-Correlation Mortality Signature?

Plain read: The finding that kicks Report #74 open. Mortality rates among fringe-topic researchers are NOT flat across topics. Anti-gravity / propulsion physics = highly elevated death rate (Eskridge, Loureiro, LeBlanc). Underground bases / black budgets = highly elevated (Schneider, Casolaro). Bioweapons = clustered elevated (2001-04 microbiologist wave). MKULTRA-adjacent = elevated (Olson, Webb). But sighting-cataloging + ancient-astronaut theory + remote viewing + abduction-narrative = ZERO elevation. Narrative researchers live to 85-90. Hardware researchers die young. That rank-ordered gradient is the engine's discriminator. TECHNICAL: Rank-ordered gradient of researcher-death rate vs proximity to operational hardware / infrastructure / classified program access. The pattern is not ideological; it is a mechanical immune-system reflex of a military-industrial complex protecting physical assets + proprietary technology vectors.
