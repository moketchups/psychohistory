What is Spatial Clearance (Foxconn Template)?

Municipal governments weaponize eminent domain to clear land for global corporations. Foxconn in Racine: $4.5B subsidies, fabricated blight declarations, fraudulent road widening, driveway severance to landlock resisters. The factory never materialized — but the land was cleared, the water rights secured, the environmental regulations exempted. Microsoft inherited it all. The political bait of human labor is the legal fiction required to bypass environmental law and achieve spatial control. The announcement IS the product.
