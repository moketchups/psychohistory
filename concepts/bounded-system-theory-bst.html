Bounded System Theory (BST)

Psychohistory Engine Concept — Plain English Explanation

What is Bounded System Theory (BST)?

No system can model its own source. Applies to physics (Planck scale), computation (context windows), cognition (you can’t see your own blind spot), and civilization (complexity collapses). The universe has a resolution limit.
