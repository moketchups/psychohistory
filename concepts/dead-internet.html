Dead Internet

Psychohistory Engine Concept — Plain English Explanation

What is Dead Internet?

The thesis that the majority of internet content and traffic is now generated by automated systems (bots, AI, scrapers), not human beings. Supporting data: 51% of web traffic is automated bots (Imperva 2024), 74.2% of indexed pages show AI-generated characteristics (Originality.ai 2025), 240+ uncensored AI tools can generate thousands of pieces of targeted content in minutes. The Dead Internet is not a conspiracy theory — it's a measurable condition. The engine tracks it as the terminal state of MindWar: when more than half of the information environment is synthetic, human agency in the information space approaches zero. The 'marketplace of ideas' requires human participants. If most participants are algorithms, the marketplace is a simulation.
