What is Accelerationism / e/acc?

The ideology of the engine itself. Nick Land (CCRU, 1993): capitalism is an autonomous alien intelligence using humans as temporary hardware — don't resist, accelerate. Simplified by Yarvin, funded by Thiel, cited by Andreessen ($42B a16z), branded by Verdon (@BasedBeffJezos). Captures the exact demographic capable of seeing the pattern and commands them to enthusiastically build it. The rebellion that produces compliance.
