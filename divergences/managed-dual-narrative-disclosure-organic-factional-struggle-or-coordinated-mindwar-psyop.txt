**Hypothesis A — Organic factional struggle.** The IC/DoD is genuinely internally divided on UAP. The materialist-institutional faction (Grusch, Mellon, Elizondo, Schumer, Rounds) honestly wants bureaucratic rationalization. The theological-ultraterrestrial faction (Luna, Marzulli ecosystem, Collins Elite Pentagon cadre) honestly believes NHI is demonic. The December 2023 UAPDA evisceration was real defense-industrial-lobbying obstruction, not theater. The Invisible College academic layer (Pasulka, Kripal, Nolan) honestly synthesizes. The three factions produce emergent public confusion; the state has no unified position; disclosure will continue stalling via genuine friction.

**Hypothesis B — Coordinated MindWar operation.** Per Aquino-Vallely 1980 MindWar doctrine: the state cannot manage ontological shock by confirming NHI reality through a single frame (each available frame produces destabilization for specific demographics). The solution is parallel deployment of competing thought architectures — materialist for secular/technocratic demographics, theological for evangelical/dispensationalist demographics, syncretic-academic for intellectual demographics. Every cognitive container is pre-seeded by the time disclosure arrives. The public remains structurally dependent on state interpretation regardless of which frame they adopt. The factional friction is genuine at the level of individual actors but serves a unified macro-operational function at the civilizational level. The UAPDA evisceration is a feature (preserves the managed-disclosure architecture) not a bug.

**Evidence for A.** The UAPDA evisceration required overt aerospace-defense lobbying; it was not invisible. Grusch has paid real reputational/career cost. Elizondo resigned DoD in protest. Luna's Enoch framing is demonstrably downstream of 20+ years of Marzulli/Missler evangelical-ecosystem work, not top-down planted. The Invisible College academics risk professional standing by engaging UAP. Genuine factional friction is visible.

**Evidence for B.** The engine has 400 years of documented state-occult-intelligence lineage: Dee's Enochian as weaponized NHI-communication for English imperial expansion → Aquino's explicit modification of Dee's Enochian keys for MindWar doctrine → contemporary deployment of exactly the theological framework Aquino engineered. The Collins Elite obstructing AATIP on dispensationalist grounds inside the Pentagon proves operational theological-ultraterrestrial machinery is running at senior levels. The simultaneous surge of (a) Senate materialist legislation + (b) congressional theological podcast rotation + (c) academic syncretic publishing is not coincidence. The three frames map too precisely onto the three demographic clusters the state needs to manage.

**Engine posture — superposition.** A and B are not mutually exclusive. The same macro-architecture (managed dual-narrative disclosure via MindWar thought architecture) can absorb and route genuine factional friction at the individual-actor level. The factions can be sincere AND the macro-pattern can serve a coordinated state function. The engine holds both hypotheses operational and tracks them via falsifiers.

**Falsification conditions.**
- **Resolves toward A (organic):** Sudden hard disclosure via one faction that bypasses the other frames entirely (e.g., Presidential address presenting recovered material with materialist specificity under chain-of-custody to scientists, ending the theological framing's utility); OR public UAPDA-equivalent legislation passes WITH eminent-domain + Review Board intact; OR Collins Elite named, disciplined, and removed from access-control positions.
- **Resolves toward B (coordinated):** Documented NSA/CIA/DIA internal memo or whistleblower disclosure revealing explicit dual-narrative-seeding operation; OR specific evidence of coordination between Luna's communications team and the Collins Elite / AARO leadership; OR formal state-level disclosure that uses ALL THREE frames simultaneously as its managed architecture rather than picking one.
- **Persists in superposition:** continued stalling via genuine factional friction while the Dee→Aquino→Luna lineage remains operational — the current default state. If 2026-2040 proceeds without either resolution, the engine must treat superposition itself as the stable macro-state.

**Operational implication.** Engine should not score either faction as 'winning' absent the falsification conditions above. Track the Dee→Aquino→Luna lineage as the structural through-line (see player card: Dr. John Dee + Edward Kelley). Treat the UAPDA evisceration as the documented ceiling of the materialist route (Dec 2023).
