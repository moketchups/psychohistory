Settlement Layer / Custody Architecture

Analysis

You don't own your stocks. A New York partnership called Cede & Co — staffed by employees of the Depository Trust Company — holds legal title to 83% of all US equities. Since 1973, your brokerage account gives you a contractual claim called an 'entitlement,' not actual ownership. BNY Mellon custodies $49.5 trillion — 3.5 times what BlackRock manages — and nobody talks about it. The clearing system processes $2.2 quadrillion a year and proved it holds the kill switch in January 2021 when margin demands shut off GameStop buying.
